Privacy Policy

Mirror — Natural Look · Android App
Last updated: March 5, 2025

Mirror — Natural Look ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we access, what we collect, and how we use it.

Camera Access

The app requires access to your device's front-facing camera solely to display your reflection on screen in real time. Camera data is never recorded, stored, uploaded, or transmitted anywhere — it is processed entirely on your device and displayed only to you.

In-App Purchases

The $5 lifetime premium upgrade is processed entirely by Google Play. We receive only a purchase token to confirm and unlock premium features. We never receive, see, or store your payment card details or billing information.

Advertising (Free Version)

The free version of the app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and anonymised usage data in order to serve relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device's Google account settings under Privacy → Ads. Premium users see no advertisements of any kind.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following information only on your device using encrypted storage:

  • Your app installation date — used only to track the 30-day free trial countdown
  • Your premium purchase status and purchase token — used only to keep you unlocked across app restarts

This data is never transmitted off your device and is never accessible to us.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not record, store, or transmit camera images or video
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or crash reporting tools

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — play.google.com/about/privacy-security-deception

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
